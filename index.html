FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers! Browse our FAQ for all the info you need

What types of photography services do you offer?

Unless you're asking us to photograph aliens, we've probably got you covered. We offer a full range of services including portraits, events, product shoots, lifestyle sessions, corporate branding, and more. Whatever your vision, we tailor the session to bring it to life.

How can I book a photography session with your studio?

If booking us feels harder than finding good lighting at noon, we’ve failed. You can easily book a session through our online form, via email, or by giving us a quick call. Once we confirm availability, we’ll lock in your date and guide you through the next steps.

Do you offer customized photography packages?

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all, unless of-course we’re talking about snacks. Yes, we offer fully customizable packages to suit your budget, style, and project needs. Just tell us what you're aiming for, and we’ll build a package that fits perfectly.

Can we view sample work or a portfolio before booking?

Of course, you wouldn’t buy a mystery box of photos… or would you? Our full portfolio is available on our website and social platforms, showcasing past shoots across different styles and categories. You’re welcome to browse to ensure our work aligns with your vision

Are your photographers experienced and skilled?

They didn't get good by accident, trust me we checked. Our photographers are trained, experienced, and passionate about capturing stunning, high-quality images. We stay updated with the latest techniques and equipment to ensure every shoot exceeds expectations.
